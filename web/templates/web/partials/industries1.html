Financial Sector

Data analytics in the finance and banking sector is mainly used in demand, supply, and risk management. Many banks are working on improving their data analytics, mainly to give them an edge against competition or to predict emerging trends that can affect their businesses. We at CRC Data & Analytics have developed products that will make bankers use their customers past data to get the future best our of their customers. Our products in this area includes:

  • Customer Returnex - which helps banks know the customers that are likely to come back to them and take loans and those that will go to other banks to take loans.
  • Risk Calculator - which helps the bank know the riskiness of their customers.
  • Sentiment Analysis - which helps banks understand the emotions and feelings of their customers on social media
